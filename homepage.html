Background and Motivation

This visualisation on plastic leakage by region is designed for researchers, students, and environmentally conscious individuals. It provides valuable data for studying plastic pollution trends, comparing regions, and tracking changes over time, aiding in research and policy recommendations. For the general public, it offers an educational tool to better understand global plastic waste challenges, raising awareness and supporting sustainability efforts.

Go To Visualisation Purpose!

Visualisation Purpose

The purpose of this visualisation is to provide insights into plastic leakage trends across regions, helping users understand the environmental impact of plastic waste. It answers key questions such as which regions experience the highest levels of plastic leakage, how trends have changed over time, and the types of plastics contributing most to pollution. It also highlights regions showing improvement in managing leakage, compares leakage levels across regions, and projects future trends.
