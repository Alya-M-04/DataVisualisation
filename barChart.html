Plastic Type

Key Takeaways

The stacked bar chart clearly shows the different amount of microplastic and macroplastic leaks over time. It emphasises that a greater portion of all plastic leaks is currently caused by macroplastics. However, the consistent rise of microplastics shows that they may become a more serious concern in the future.

Graph Justification:

  • Stacked bar charts are ideal for comparing different categories, such as microplastics and macroplastics, throughout time periods.
  • The stacked structure efficiently highlights total plastic leakage while also splitting it down into each of its parts, offering insights into both the overall trend and individual contributions.
  • The different bar heights highlight changes in magnitude between years and categories, making it simple to discover growth trends and potential abnormalities.
