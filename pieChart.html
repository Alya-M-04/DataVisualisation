Key Takeaways

The pie chart provides an easy-to-understand way to understand the distribution of plastic types by efficiently visualising the percentage of various plastic categories (such as microplastics and macroplastics) within a particular region and year. In addition to improving data exploration with interactive elements like tooltips, it enables users to quickly figure out which category makes up the majority of the overall plastic data.

Graph Justification:

  • Clear Proportions: Makes data easier to interpret by showing how much of each type of plastic contributes to the total.
  • Interactivity: By offering more information when hovered over, tooltips improve user engagement.
  • Suitable for Small Categories: Perfect for showing small categories (Microplastics, Macroplastics).
  • Simplicity: Provides a user-friendly format that enables fast data exploration and interpretation.
